# Cancellation Policy

## Canceling Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard.

Steps:

1. Go to Settings
2. Click "Billing"
3. Select "Cancel Subscription"

## What Happens After Cancellation

* Your plan remains active until the end of the billing cycle
* No further charges will be applied

## Immediate Termination

Immediate account termination is **not standard** and requires support intervention.

## Refunds After Cancellation

Canceling a subscription does **not automatically trigger a refund**.
Refer to the Refund Policy for eligibility.

## Support Notes

* If a user requests both cancellation and refund, escalate for review
* Do not confirm refund eligibility without checking policy conditions
