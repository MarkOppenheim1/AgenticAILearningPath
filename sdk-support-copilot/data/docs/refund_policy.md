# Refund Policy

## Overview

We offer refunds under specific conditions depending on the subscription type and usage.

## Eligibility

* Monthly plans: refundable within **7 days** of purchase
* Annual plans: refundable within **14 days** of purchase
* Refunds are **not available** after significant usage of the service

## Non-Refundable Cases

* Accounts that have processed more than 1,000 API calls
* Accounts flagged for abuse or policy violations
* Partial refunds for unused time are **not supported**

## How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

1. Contact support with your account email
2. Provide the reason for the request
3. Include your invoice ID (if available)

## Processing Time

* Refunds are processed within **5–10 business days**
* Funds are returned to the original payment method

## Important Notes

* Refund requests may be reviewed manually
* Support agents should **not promise refunds** without approval if the case is unclear
