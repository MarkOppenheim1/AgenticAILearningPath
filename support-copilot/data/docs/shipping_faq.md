# Shipping FAQ

## Delivery Times

* Standard shipping: 5–7 business days
* Express shipping: 2–3 business days

## Delayed Shipments

Delays may occur due to:

* Weather conditions
* Carrier issues
* High demand periods

## Lost Packages

If your package has not arrived within 10 business days:

* Contact support
* Provide your tracking number

## Compensation

We do **not guarantee compensation** for delayed shipments.
Compensation requests should be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

## Address Changes

Shipping addresses cannot be changed after the order has been processed.
