# Account Management

## Resetting Your Password

To reset your password:

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Follow the email instructions

## Changing Email Address

* Users can update their email in account settings
* Verification is required for security

## Deleting an Account

Account deletion requests must be submitted through support.

Important:

* This action is **irreversible**
* All data will be permanently deleted

## Security Notes

* Never share passwords
* Support agents should not request sensitive credentials
