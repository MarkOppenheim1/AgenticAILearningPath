# Billing FAQ

## Accepted Payment Methods

* Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
* PayPal

## Billing Cycle

* Monthly or annual billing options available
* Charges occur at the start of each billing cycle

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails:

* The system retries within 3 days
* Access may be limited after repeated failures

## Invoice Requests

Users can download invoices from the billing section.

## Plan Changes

* Upgrades take effect immediately
* Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle

## Support Notes

* Billing disputes should be handled carefully
* Escalate cases involving refunds or chargebacks
